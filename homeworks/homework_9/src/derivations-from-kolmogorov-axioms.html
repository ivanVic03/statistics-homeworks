Module 4: Formal Derivations from Kolmogorov’s Axioms

4.1 Preliminary Results

Empty Set: \(P(\emptyset)=0\).

Complement: \(P(A^c)=1-P(A)\).

Monotonicity: If \(A\subseteq B\) then \(P(A)\le P(B)\).

4.2 Subadditivity (Boole’s Inequality)

\[ P\left(\bigcup_{i=1}^{\infty} A_i\right) \le \sum_{i=1}^{\infty} P(A_i) \]
