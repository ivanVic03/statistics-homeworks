Trajectories
This chart shows how the relative frequency of successes evolves during each trajectory.
Each line starts at zero and fluctuates as trials accumulate.
The line goes up after a success and may stabilize around the true probability p as more trials are run.
Use the “Automatic scroll” button to explore all trajectories one by one.
Notice how individual paths differ at first but tend to cluster near p over time — this illustrates convergence in probability.
Histogram of Final Successes
This histogram shows the final relative frequencies of success across all simulated trajectories.
Each bar represents how many trajectories ended within a specific range of frequencies.
Most trajectories cluster around the true probability p, forming a bell-like shape.
As the number of trials increases, the distribution becomes tighter around p — a clear sign of the Law of Large Numbers in action.
