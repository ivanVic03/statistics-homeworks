Online Algorithms: Mean & Variance

Introduction

In this project, we explore online algorithms for calculating the mean and variance of a dataset. Unlike traditional formulas, these algorithms update the values incrementally as new data arrives.

Advantages

Online algorithms are memory-efficient and suitable for streaming data or large datasets. They avoid storing all data and compute updates in real-time.
