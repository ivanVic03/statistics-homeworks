Arithmetic Mean

The arithmetic mean is the sum of all values divided by their count. Ideal for additive processes, but sensitive to outliers.

Example: {5, 10, 15} → (5 + 10 + 15) / 3 = 10

Geometric Mean

The nth root of the product of positive values. Essential for growth and compounding.

Example: 1.5, 0.5 → √(1.5 × 0.5) − 1 ≈ −0.134

Weighted Mean

Each value is multiplied by its weight and divided by the total of the weights.

Example: 10:100, 40:50 → (1000 + 2000) / 150 = 20
